# ICP Scoring Rubric — Export Arena

## Target Profile

Export Arena automates import/export operations using AI. Ideal customers:
- Active in international trade (import/export)
- 50-500 employees (mid-market sweet spot)
- Manual processes ripe for automation
- Budget for automation tools ($2K-$20K/mo)

## Scoring (1-10)

| Factor | Weight | 8-10 (Hot) | 5-7 (Warm) | 1-4 (Cold) |
|--------|--------|------------|------------|------------|
| **Trade Activity** | 30% | Active exporter/importer | Domestic with supply chain | No trade activity |
| **Company Size** | 20% | 50-500 employees | 10-50 or 500-2000 | <10 or >2000 |
| **AI Readiness** | 15% | Uses automation/AI tools | Tech-forward, no AI yet | Low-tech |
| **Decision Maker** | 15% | C-suite/VP direct contact | Director/Manager | Unknown/Junior |
| **Industry Fit** | 10% | Manufacturing, logistics, FMCG | Tech, agriculture, pharma | Government, NGO |
| **Budget Signals** | 10% | Recent funding, growth mode | Stable, profitable | Cost-cutting, layoffs |

## Tier Mapping

- **Hot (8-10):** Immediate outreach. Assign to senior AE.
- **Warm (5-7):** Nurture sequence. Add to drip campaign.
- **Cold (1-4):** Archive. Revisit quarterly.

## Israeli Market Bonuses

- +1 if company is Israeli (Export Arena's home market)
- +1 if active in Israel-EU or Israel-US trade corridors
- +1 if mentioned in Israeli trade publications
