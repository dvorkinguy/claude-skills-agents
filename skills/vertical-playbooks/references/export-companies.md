# Export Companies Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- Companies exporting goods internationally
- B2B focus (selling to foreign buyers/distributors)
- Deal with customs, shipping, documentation
- Sweet spot: 10-500 shipments/month

**Key Decision Makers:**
- Export Manager
- Operations Director
- Compliance Officer
- Founder/Owner (SMEs)

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Export Documentation Chaos
**Symptoms:**
- Manual document preparation
- Errors causing shipment delays
- Customs rejections
- Certificate hunting

**Questions:**
- "How do you prepare export documents today?"
- "How many documents per shipment on average?"
- "What happens when a document has an error?"
- "How long does document preparation take?"

**Automation:**
- Auto-generate packing lists from orders
- Commercial invoice generation
- Certificate of origin workflow
- Document checklist by destination country
- Customs form auto-fill

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Shipments/month × Hours per document set × Hourly rate
Plus: Delays from doc errors × Cost per delay
Example: 50 shipments × 2 hours × $40 = $4,000/month
```

---

### 2. Customer Order Management
**Symptoms:**
- Orders via email/WhatsApp
- Manual quote preparation
- No visibility on order status
- Payment collection issues

**Questions:**
- "How do export orders come in?"
- "How long to prepare a quote?"
- "How do customers check order status?"
- "What's your payment terms enforcement?"

**Automation:**
- Order intake → CRM/ERP entry
- Quote generation templates
- Order status portal for buyers
- Payment milestone alerts
- Proforma to commercial invoice flow

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Quotes/month × Time to prepare × Hourly rate
Plus: Lost orders from slow response
```

---

### 3. Shipping & Logistics Coordination
**Symptoms:**
- Chasing freight forwarders
- Container/vessel tracking manually
- ETD/ETA updates by phone
- Multi-party communication gaps

**Questions:**
- "How do you track shipments currently?"
- "How often do buyers ask for status?"
- "How do you coordinate with freight forwarders?"
- "What happens when there's a delay?"

**Automation:**
- Shipping milestone tracking
- Auto-updates to buyers (sailed/arrived/customs)
- Freight forwarder integration
- Delay alerts with proactive communication
- Container tracking dashboard

**ROI Calculation:**
```
"Where's my shipment" inquiries × Time per inquiry × Hourly rate
Plus: Customer satisfaction impact
```

---

### 4. Compliance & Regulations
**Symptoms:**
- Keeping up with regulations
- Export license tracking
- Restricted party screening
- Dual-use goods classification

**Questions:**
- "How do you handle export compliance?"
- "Do you screen against restricted party lists?"
- "How do you track export licenses?"
- "Any compliance close-calls?"

**Automation:**
- Denied party screening on new orders
- Export license expiry alerts
- Country-specific document checklist
- Compliance audit trail
- Regulation update notifications

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Compliance risk × Potential fine + Hours on manual compliance × Hourly rate
```

## Key Integrations

### Common Stack
- **ERP:** SAP, NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics
- **Shipping:** Flexport API, Freightos, CargoWise
- **Documents:** DocuSign, PandaDoc
- **Tracking:** MarineTraffic, VesselFinder, project44
- **Compliance:** Descartes, Amber Road, Visual Compliance
- **Banking:** SWIFT, TradeIX, Komgo

### Document Types
| Document | Automation Level |
|----------|------------------|
| Commercial Invoice | High - auto-generate |
| Packing List | High - from order data |
| Bill of Lading | Medium - template + review |
| Certificate of Origin | Medium - pre-fill, manual sign |
| Export Declaration | Medium - depends on country |
| Insurance Certificate | Low - usually from insurer |

## Country-Specific Considerations

### EU Exports
- EUR1/ATR certificates
- EORI number requirements
- VAT documentation
- Intrastat reporting

### US Exports
- EEI filing (AES)
- Export license requirements
- ECCN classification
- Deemed exports

### Asia-Pacific
- Form A for GSP
- Country-specific certificates
- Legalization requirements
- Multiple language requirements

## Common Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
