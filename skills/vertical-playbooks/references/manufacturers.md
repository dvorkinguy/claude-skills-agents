# Manufacturers Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- Production facilities making physical goods
- Can be discrete or process manufacturing
- B2B (industrial) or B2C (consumer goods)
- Sweet spot: 20-500 employees, $5M-100M revenue

**Key Decision Makers:**
- Plant Manager
- Operations Director
- Production Manager
- Quality Manager
- Supply Chain Manager

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Production Visibility Gaps
**Symptoms:**
- "Where is my order?" from sales/customers
- Manual production tracking
- Scheduling conflicts
- No real-time status

**Questions:**
- "How do you track production status today?"
- "How does sales know when orders will ship?"
- "How do you handle schedule changes?"
- "What visibility do customers have?"

**Automation:**
- Production milestone tracking
- Auto-updates to sales/customers
- Schedule conflict alerts
- WIP (work-in-progress) dashboards
- Completion notifications

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Status inquiry calls × Time per call × Hourly rate
Plus: Expedite costs from poor visibility
```

---

### 2. Quality Control Issues
**Symptoms:**
- Manual inspection logging
- Quality reports after the fact
- Traceability headaches during recalls
- Non-conformance tracking gaps

**Questions:**
- "How do you record quality inspections?"
- "How quickly can you trace a batch if there's a problem?"
- "What happens when a quality issue is found?"
- "How do you track non-conformances?"

**Automation:**
- Digital inspection forms
- Real-time quality alerts
- Non-conformance workflow
- Batch traceability database
- Quality metrics dashboards

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Quality escapes × Cost per escape + Recall readiness value
Plus: Time on manual quality paperwork
```

---

### 3. Inventory & Material Management
**Symptoms:**
- Production stops from missing materials
- Manual inventory counts
- Obsolete/excess inventory
- Supplier coordination issues

**Questions:**
- "How often does production stop due to materials?"
- "How do you manage raw material inventory?"
- "How do you coordinate with suppliers?"
- "What's your biggest inventory headache?"

**Automation:**
- Low material alerts
- Auto-reorder triggers
- Supplier PO automation
- Receiving confirmation workflow
- Inventory reconciliation

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Downtime from material shortages × Cost per hour of downtime
Plus: Excess inventory carrying cost savings
```

---

### 4. Maintenance & Equipment
**Symptoms:**
- Unplanned downtime
- Manual maintenance logs
- No predictive capability
- Spare parts unavailable

**Questions:**
- "How do you schedule maintenance?"
- "What's your unplanned downtime rate?"
- "How do you track equipment issues?"
- "How do you manage spare parts?"

**Automation:**
- Preventive maintenance scheduling
- Maintenance due alerts
- Downtime logging and alerts
- Spare parts inventory integration
- Equipment performance tracking

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Unplanned downtime hours × Cost per hour
Plus: Maintenance staff efficiency gains
```

## Key Integrations

### Common Stack
- **ERP:** SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, Epicor, SYSPRO
- **MES:** Plex, IQMS (DELMIAWorks), Prodsmart
- **Quality:** Qualio, MasterControl, ETQ
- **Maintenance:** UpKeep, Fiix, MaintenanceConnection
- **Inventory:** Fishbowl, inFlow, Katana
- **PLM:** Arena, Propel, Teamcenter

### Manufacturing Data Types
| Data Type | Source | Automation |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Work Orders | ERP/MES | Track + update |
| Inspections | Quality forms | Capture + route |
| Machine Status | PLC/SCADA | Monitor + alert |
| Inventory Levels | WMS/ERP | Monitor + reorder |
| Maintenance | CMMS | Schedule + track |
