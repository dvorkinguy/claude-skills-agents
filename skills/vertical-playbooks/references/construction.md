# Construction Companies Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- General contractors and specialty contractors
- Residential, commercial, or industrial construction
- Companies with multiple active job sites
- Sweet spot: $5M-100M revenue, 10-300 employees

**Key Decision Makers:**
- Owner/Principal
- Operations Manager
- Project Manager
- Superintendent
- Office Manager

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Field-to-Office Communication Gap
**Symptoms:**
- Superintendents buried in email/calls
- No real-time job status
- End-of-day scramble for updates
- Information lost in texts/calls

**Questions:**
- "How do field teams report back to office?"
- "How do you know what happened on site today?"
- "How do PMs get updates from supers?"
- "What information gets lost between field and office?"

**Automation:**
- Daily report automation
- Photo documentation workflow
- Issue/RFI alerts
- Weather delay notifications
- Progress milestone updates

**ROI Calculation:**
```
PMs × Hours/day on status updates × Hourly rate
Plus: Cost of delayed decisions from poor info
```

---

### 2. Change Order Chaos
**Symptoms:**
- Verbal changes not documented
- Unbilled extra work
- Disputed scope
- Change orders created weeks later

**Questions:**
- "How do you track changes in scope?"
- "How quickly do change orders get written?"
- "How much work goes unbilled?"
- "What's your change order dispute rate?"

**Automation:**
- Field change notification
- Auto-draft change order
- Approval workflow
- Customer notification
- Billing integration

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Unbilled change work/year × Recovery rate with automation
Example: $100k unbilled × 80% recovery = $80k
```

---

### 3. Subcontractor Coordination
**Symptoms:**
- Scheduling conflicts
- No-shows without notice
- Quality issues discovered late
- Insurance/compliance tracking nightmare

**Questions:**
- "How do you schedule subcontractors?"
- "How often do subs miss schedules?"
- "How do you verify sub compliance (insurance, licenses)?"
- "How do you evaluate sub performance?"

**Automation:**
- Schedule confirmation requests
- Insurance expiry tracking
- Performance scoring
- Sub communication hub
- No-show alerts

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Schedule disruptions × Cost per disruption
Plus: Compliance risk reduction value
```

---

### 4. Safety & Compliance Tracking
**Symptoms:**
- Paper safety forms
- Inspection scheduling chaos
- Compliance documentation gaps
- Incident reporting delays

**Questions:**
- "How do you handle safety documentation?"
- "How do you schedule inspections?"
- "How do you track certifications and training?"
- "What happens when there's an incident?"

**Automation:**
- Digital safety forms (daily/weekly)
- Inspection request automation
- Certification expiry alerts
- Incident reporting workflow
- OSHA-ready documentation

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Safety incidents reduced × Cost per incident
Plus: Compliance fine avoidance
```

## Key Integrations

### Common Stack
- **Project Management:** Procore, Buildertrend, CoConstruct, BuilderTREND
- **Estimating:** Bluebeam, PlanSwift, ConstructConnect
- **Accounting:** QuickBooks, Sage, Foundation
- **Scheduling:** Microsoft Project, Primavera, Smartsheet
- **Safety:** Safety Reports, iAuditor
- **Communication:** Fieldwire, Plangrid, Raken

### Construction Data Types
| Data | Source | Automation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Daily Reports | Field app | Compile + distribute |
| Change Orders | Field notification | Draft + route |
| RFIs | PM/Architect | Track + route |
| Submittals | Subs | Track + approve |
| Inspections | Field/Inspector | Schedule + document |
| Safety Forms | Field | Collect + analyze |
