# Global Trade Professional Services Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- Customs brokers and freight forwarders
- Trade compliance consultants
- International trade lawyers
- Trade finance advisors
- Export/import consultants
- Sweet spot: 10-100 employees, multi-client operations

**Key Decision Makers:**
- Managing Director/Owner
- Operations Manager
- Compliance Director
- Business Development Lead
- Senior Consultant/Broker

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Client Onboarding Overload
**Symptoms:**
- Manual data collection from new clients
- Power of attorney delays
- Missing required information
- Repeated requests for same documents

**Questions:**
- "How do you onboard new import/export clients?"
- "How long from first contact to first transaction?"
- "What documents do you collect from every client?"
- "How do you handle clients with multiple entities?"

**Automation:**
- Digital onboarding portal
- Document request automation
- Power of attorney workflow
- Entity structure mapping
- Compliance profile building

**ROI Calculation:**
```
New clients/month × Hours per onboarding × Hourly rate
Plus: Lost clients from slow onboarding × Client lifetime value
```

---

### 2. Transaction Processing Bottleneck
**Symptoms:**
- Manual data entry for entries/declarations
- Chasing clients for documents
- High error rates requiring corrections
- Staff capacity limits growth

**Questions:**
- "How many entries/transactions do you process daily?"
- "How much is manual vs. automated?"
- "What's your error/rejection rate?"
- "How do you handle peak volumes?"

**Automation:**
- Document extraction (OCR/AI)
- Data validation before submission
- Client notification automation
- Exception handling workflow
- Quality check integration

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Transactions × Time per transaction × Hourly rate × Time reduction %
Plus: Error corrections avoided × Cost per correction
```

---

### 3. Client Communication Chaos
**Symptoms:**
- Clients asking "what's the status?"
- Manual status updates
- Same questions from different stakeholders
- Information trapped in emails

**Questions:**
- "How do clients check their shipment status?"
- "How many status update requests do you get daily?"
- "How do you communicate exceptions?"
- "How do clients access their documents?"

**Automation:**
- Client portal with real-time status
- Proactive milestone notifications
- Exception alerts before client asks
- Document availability notifications
- Self-service document access

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Status inquiries × Time per inquiry × Hourly rate
Plus: Client satisfaction impact on retention
```

---

### 4. Compliance & Audit Trail Gaps
**Symptoms:**
- Documentation scattered across systems
- Hard to prove compliance for audits
- Client-specific requirements forgotten
- Regulation changes missed

**Questions:**
- "How do you prepare for customs audits?"
- "How do you track client-specific requirements?"
- "How do you stay current on regulation changes?"
- "What's your record retention process?"

**Automation:**
- Centralized transaction archive
- Audit-ready report generation
- Client profile alerts (requirements)
- Regulation update monitoring
- Document retention automation

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Audit prep hours × Frequency × Hourly rate
Plus: Compliance risk reduction value
```

**Time to deliver:** 1-2 days

   - Milestone notifications to clients
   - Exception alerts
   - Delivery confirmation

2. **Document Request Automation**
   - Standard request templates
   - Reminders if missing
   - Receipt confirmation

## Common Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Customs systems are the bottleneck" | "We optimize everything around the customs filing - the prep, the follow-up, the client communication." |
| "Our software handles this" | "We connect to your software and automate what it can't - client communication, document collection, exceptions." |
| "Clients expect personal service" | "They'll get more personal attention when you're not buried in data entry and status updates." |
| "Every client is different" | "We build profiles per client and customize automation to their specific needs." |
| "We're a small operation" | "Small teams need efficiency even more - every hour counts when you're lean." |
| "Our margins are tight" | "Tighter margins mean efficiency is survival. Automation lets you do more without hiring more." |
