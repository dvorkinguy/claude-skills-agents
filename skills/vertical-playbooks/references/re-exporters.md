# Re-Exporters Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- Companies that import goods and re-export (with or without modification)
- Free trade zone (FTZ) operators
- Transit trade specialists
- Triangular trade operators
- Sweet spot: 30-300 shipments/month

**Key Decision Makers:**
- Operations Director
- Trade Compliance Manager
- Logistics Manager
- CFO (finance/FX exposure)
- Founder/Owner (trading houses)

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Complex Trade Documentation
**Symptoms:**
- Multiple origin/destination document sets
- Re-export license tracking
- FTZ compliance paperwork
- Certificate of origin transformations

**Questions:**
- "How do you manage documentation for both import and export?"
- "How do you handle re-export license requirements?"
- "How do you track country of origin through re-export?"
- "What FTZ documentation do you maintain?"

**Automation:**
- Import/export document linking
- Re-export license tracking and alerts
- Origin certificate workflow
- FTZ entry/exit documentation
- Compliance checklist by route

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Shipments × Documentation time × Hourly rate
Plus: Compliance errors × Cost per error (delays, fines)
```

---

### 2. Triangular Trade Coordination
**Symptoms:**
- Three-party communication chaos
- Payment flow complexity
- Different document requirements per party
- Timing mismatches

**Questions:**
- "How do you coordinate between supplier, buyer, and yourself?"
- "How do you manage back-to-back LCs?"
- "How do you track goods you never physically see?"
- "What happens when there's a discrepancy?"

**Automation:**
- Multi-party status tracking
- Document flow management
- Payment milestone tracking
- Discrepancy alerts
- Party-specific notifications

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Hours/week on coordination × Hourly rate × 4.33
Plus: Cost of timing mismatches × Frequency
```

---

### 3. Inventory in Transit/FTZ
**Symptoms:**
- No visibility on goods in transit
- FTZ inventory tracking issues
- Value-added processing tracking
- Dwell time management

**Questions:**
- "How do you track inventory in transit?"
- "How do you manage FTZ dwell time limits?"
- "What value-added activities do you do?"
- "How do you track transformation processes?"

**Automation:**
- In-transit inventory dashboard
- FTZ entry/exit tracking
- Dwell time alerts
- Value-added activity logging
- Zone inventory reconciliation

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Dwell time penalties avoided + Inventory carrying cost reduction
Plus: Staff time on manual tracking
```

---

### 4. Financial & FX Management
**Symptoms:**
- Back-to-back payment timing issues
- FX exposure from timing gaps
- Margin erosion from poor FX
- Cash flow management

**Questions:**
- "How do you manage FX exposure on re-export margins?"
- "How do you time payments with receipts?"
- "How do you track profitability per shipment?"
- "What's your margin erosion from FX?"

**Automation:**
- Payment matching alerts
- FX rate monitoring
- Margin calculator per shipment
- Cash flow forecasting
- Payment timing optimization

**ROI Calculation:**
```
FX losses avoided + Cash flow optimization value
```

## Key Integrations

### Common Stack
- **Trade Management:** CargoWise, Descartes
- **FTZ Systems:** Zone management software
- **Shipping:** Multiple forwarder APIs
- **Banking:** Trade finance platforms
- **ERP:** SAP GTS, Oracle GTM
- **Compliance:** Visual Compliance, Amber Road

### Document Flow Types
| Document | Inbound | Outbound |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Commercial Invoice | From supplier | New for buyer |
| Packing List | From supplier | May modify |
| Bill of Lading | From carrier | New BOL |
| Certificate of Origin | From supplier | New if transformed |
| Insurance | May transfer | New if required |
| Customs Entry | Import entry | Export declaration |

## Compliance Considerations

### Re-Export Controls
- US EAR re-export rules
- EU dual-use regulations
- Origin determination after processing
- License requirements by end-destination

### FTZ Compliance
- Weekly/monthly entry summaries
- Zone inventory accuracy
- Privileged foreign status
- Manufacturing drawback

### Sanctions Screening
- Screen both supplier and end-buyer
- Transit country considerations
- End-use/end-user verification
- Diversion risk assessment
