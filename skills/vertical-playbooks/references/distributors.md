# Distributors Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- Companies that buy and resell goods (no manufacturing)
- Wholesale, B2B distribution, trading companies
- Multiple suppliers, multiple customers
- Sweet spot: $2M-100M revenue, 1000+ SKUs

**Key Decision Makers:**
- General Manager
- Operations Manager
- Sales Director
- Purchasing Manager
- Warehouse Manager

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Order Processing Bottleneck
**Symptoms:**
- Manual order entry from emails/fax/phone
- Pricing errors and disputes
- Slow quote turnaround
- Customer complaints about response time

**Questions:**
- "How do orders come in today?"
- "How long from order to confirmation?"
- "What causes pricing errors?"
- "How do customers check availability?"

**Automation:**
- Multi-channel order intake
- Auto-populate from customer history
- Real-time pricing lookup
- Inventory availability check
- Order confirmation automation

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Orders/day × Manual processing time × Hourly rate
Plus: Lost orders from slow response × Average order value
```

---

### 2. Inventory Visibility Chaos
**Symptoms:**
- Don't know what's in stock
- Overselling and backorders
- Can't find items in warehouse
- Stock counts are wrong

**Questions:**
- "How accurate is your inventory?"
- "How often do you oversell?"
- "How do you know what to reorder?"
- "How often do customers get backorder notices?"

**Automation:**
- Real-time inventory sync
- Multi-location visibility
- Low stock alerts
- Reorder point notifications
- Cycle count scheduling

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Backorders/month × Cost per backorder (lost margin + handling)
Plus: Stockouts × Lost sales value
```

---

### 3. Supplier Management
**Symptoms:**
- Chasing suppliers for delivery dates
- No visibility on incoming inventory
- Price change surprises
- Poor supplier performance visibility

**Questions:**
- "How do you track supplier orders?"
- "How often do suppliers miss delivery dates?"
- "How do you know what's on its way?"
- "How do you evaluate suppliers?"

**Automation:**
- PO tracking and reminders
- ETA integration
- Price change alerts
- Supplier scorecard automation
- Inbound inventory visibility

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Hours/week on supplier follow-up × Hourly rate × 4.33
Plus: Cost of surprise delays × Frequency
```

---

### 4. Customer Service Overload
**Symptoms:**
- "Where's my order?" calls
- Pricing and availability questions
- Manual status updates
- Same questions over and over

**Questions:**
- "What do customers ask most often?"
- "How do customers get order status?"
- "How do you handle availability checks?"
- "What's your quote-to-order conversion?"

**Automation:**
- Customer portal for self-service
- Auto-status updates
- Availability check automation
- Quote follow-up sequences
- FAQ chatbot

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Customer service calls × Cost per call
Plus: Hours on manual updates × Hourly rate
```

## Key Integrations

### Common Stack
- **ERP:** NetSuite, SAP Business One, Acumatica, QuickBooks Enterprise
- **WMS:** Fishbowl, 3PL systems, RF scanning
- **E-commerce:** Shopify Plus, BigCommerce B2B, Magento
- **CRM:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho
- **Shipping:** ShipStation, UPS/FedEx APIs
- **EDI:** SPS Commerce, TrueCommerce

### Data Flow Types
| Data | Direction | Integration |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Orders | Inbound | Email/Portal → ERP |
| Inventory | Outbound | ERP → Website/Portal |
| Shipments | Outbound | WMS → Customer |
| Invoices | Outbound | ERP → Customer |
| POs | Outbound | ERP → Supplier |
| Receipts | Inbound | WMS → ERP |
