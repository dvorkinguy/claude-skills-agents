# Import Companies Playbook

## Overview

**Target Profile:**
- Companies importing goods for resale or manufacturing
- Deal with suppliers, customs, inventory management
- Compliance-sensitive (customs, duties, standards)
- Sweet spot: 20-300 shipments/month

**Key Decision Makers:**
- Import Manager/Procurement Manager
- Supply Chain Director
- Customs Compliance Officer
- Operations Director

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Supplier Communication Chaos
**Symptoms:**
- Chasing suppliers for production updates
- PO confirmations lost in email
- No visibility until goods ship
- Quality issues discovered too late

**Questions:**
- "How do you track supplier production status?"
- "How often do you chase suppliers for updates?"
- "What happens when a supplier is delayed?"
- "How do you handle quality control before shipping?"

**Automation:**
- PO acknowledgment tracking
- Production milestone reminders
- Pre-shipment inspection scheduling
- Supplier performance tracking
- Delay alerts to internal teams

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Hours/week chasing suppliers × Hourly rate × 4.33
Plus: Cost of surprise delays × Frequency
```

---

### 2. Customs & Clearance Delays
**Symptoms:**
- Documents missing at arrival
- HS code classification issues
- Duty miscalculations
- Goods stuck at port

**Questions:**
- "How often do shipments get delayed at customs?"
- "How do you prepare for customs clearance?"
- "Who handles HS code classification?"
- "What's your average clearance time?"

**Automation:**
- Pre-arrival document checklist
- Customs broker notifications
- Duty calculation estimates
- Clearance status tracking
- Exception alerts

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Clearance delays/month × Storage/demurrage cost per day × Average delay days
Plus: Staff time on customs issues
```

---

### 3. Inventory & Arrival Planning
**Symptoms:**
- Stockouts from late arrivals
- No visibility on incoming inventory
- Manual ETA tracking
- Planning based on guesswork

**Questions:**
- "How do you plan for incoming inventory?"
- "How often do you run out of stock waiting for arrivals?"
- "How accurate are your ETAs?"
- "How do you plan production/sales around arrivals?"

**Automation:**
- Shipment tracking integration
- ETA updates to planning systems
- Low stock + incoming inventory alerts
- Arrival scheduling automation
- Warehouse preparation notifications

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Stockouts/year × Lost revenue per stockout
Plus: Expedited shipping costs from poor planning
```

---

### 4. Cost & Payment Management
**Symptoms:**
- Landed cost surprises
- Payment timing issues
- Foreign exchange management
- Duty/tax reconciliation headaches

**Questions:**
- "How do you calculate landed costs?"
- "How do you manage supplier payments (LC, TT)?"
- "How do you handle FX risk?"
- "What's your duty drawback situation?"

**Automation:**
- Landed cost calculator
- Payment milestone tracking
- LC expiry alerts
- FX rate monitoring
- Duty/tax documentation for drawback

**ROI Calculation:**
```
Hours/month on cost calculations × Hourly rate
Plus: FX losses from poor timing × Volume
```

## Key Integrations

### Common Stack
- **ERP:** SAP, NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics, QuickBooks
- **Inventory:** Cin7, TradeGecko, Fishbowl, DEAR
- **Shipping:** Flexport, Freightos, project44
- **Customs:** customs broker systems, ABI/ACE (US), CHIEF (UK)
- **Banking:** Trade finance platforms, FX providers
- **Quality:** InspectALL, QIMA integration

### Import Document Types
| Document | Source | Automation |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Commercial Invoice | Supplier | Receive + verify |
| Packing List | Supplier | Receive + match to PO |
| Bill of Lading | Forwarder/Carrier | Track + store |
| Certificate of Origin | Supplier | Verify + store |
| Quality Certificates | Supplier/Inspector | Track + verify |
| Customs Entry | Broker | Receive + reconcile |

## Compliance Considerations

### Common Import Regulations
- HS code classification accuracy
- Country of origin verification
- Anti-dumping duty checks

- Licensing requirements
- Restricted party screening

### Automation Safeguards
- Classification database
- Duty rate lookups
- Certificate expiry tracking
- Audit trail for all documents
- Alert on regulation changes
