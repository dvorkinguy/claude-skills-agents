## Validation Checklist

Before launching, confirm:

- [ ] Solves a real problem I've seen repeatedly
- [ ] Simple enough to remember (3-5 steps max)
- [ ] Actionable - people can apply it immediately
- [ ] Unique - different from existing frameworks
- [ ] Proven - I've used this successfully
- [ ] Nameable - the name is memorable
