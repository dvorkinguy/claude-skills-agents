# Brand Archetypes

The 12 archetypes help define your brand personality and messaging.

---

## The 12 Archetypes

### 1. The Sage
**Core desire:** Find truth and wisdom
**Voice:** Thoughtful, analytical, educational
**Messaging:** "Here's what the data shows..."
**Personal brands:** Naval Ravikant, Tim Ferriss

### 2. The Creator
**Core desire:** Create something of enduring value
**Voice:** Innovative, expressive, visionary
**Messaging:** "Let me show you what's possible..."
**Personal brands:** Casey Neistat, Marie Forleo

### 3. The Rebel (Outlaw)
**Core desire:** Revolution, break the rules
**Voice:** Provocative, bold, unapologetic
**Messaging:** "Everything you've been told is wrong..."
**Personal brands:** Gary Vaynerchuk, Grant Cardone

### 4. The Hero
**Core desire:** Prove worth through courageous action
**Voice:** Inspiring, determined, empowering
**Messaging:** "You can overcome this..."
**Personal brands:** Tony Robbins, David Goggins

### 5. The Magician
**Core desire:** Make dreams come true
**Voice:** Transformative, visionary, catalytic
**Messaging:** "Imagine what would happen if..."
**Personal brands:** Seth Godin, Oprah Winfrey

### 6. The Caregiver
**Core desire:** Protect and care for others
**Voice:** Nurturing, supportive, generous
**Messaging:** "I'm here to help you..."
**Personal brands:** Brene Brown, Simon Sinek

### 7. The Everyman
**Core desire:** Belonging, connection
**Voice:** Relatable, down-to-earth, friendly
**Messaging:** "I'm just like you, and here's what worked..."
**Personal brands:** Pat Flynn, Amy Porterfield

### 8. The Lover
**Core desire:** Intimacy and experience
**Voice:** Passionate, sensual, appreciative
**Messaging:** "Fall in love with [your craft]..."
**Personal brands:** Lifestyle creators, relationship coaches

### 9. The Jester
**Core desire:** Live in the moment with enjoyment
**Voice:** Playful, witty, irreverent
**Messaging:** "Who says [topic] has to be boring?"
**Personal brands:** Ryan Reynolds, Comedic creators

### 10. The Ruler
**Core desire:** Control, create order
**Voice:** Authoritative, commanding, confident
**Messaging:** "Here's how to take charge..."
**Personal brands:** Business moguls, executive coaches

### 11. The Innocent
**Core desire:** Be happy, experience paradise
**Voice:** Optimistic, pure, trusting
**Messaging:** "It can be simple..."
**Personal brands:** Wellness creators, minimalists

### 12. The Explorer
**Core desire:** Freedom to discover
**Voice:** Adventurous, independent, pioneering
**Messaging:** "Let's discover what's out there..."
**Personal brands:** Travel creators, digital nomads

## Find Your Archetype

### Quick Assessment

Answer honestly:

1. **What drives you most?**
   - Teaching/wisdom → Sage
   - Creating/building → Creator
   - Challenging status quo → Rebel
   - Inspiring action → Hero
   - Transformation → Magician
   - Helping others → Caregiver

2. **How do people describe you?**
   - "You always make me think" → Sage
   - "You're so creative" → Creator
   - "You're fearless" → Rebel
   - "You inspire me" → Hero
   - "You changed my perspective" → Magician
   - "You're so supportive" → Caregiver

3. **What's your natural content style?**
   - Research-based, analytical → Sage
   - Visual, innovative → Creator
   - Controversial, bold → Rebel
   - Challenge-focused → Hero
   - Vision-focused → Magician
   - Community-focused → Caregiver

## Archetype Messaging Guide

### Sage Content

**Headlines:**
- "The Data-Driven Guide to [Topic]"
- "What Research Tells Us About [Topic]"
- "The Hidden Truth About [Topic]"

**Voice traits:** Thoughtful, measured, evidence-based

**Avoid:** Hype, unsubstantiated claims

### Creator Content

**Headlines:**
- "How I Built [Thing] From Scratch"
- "A New Approach to [Topic]"
- "The Future of [Industry]"

**Voice traits:** Visionary, experimental, original

**Avoid:** Following trends, playing it safe

### Rebel Content

**Headlines:**
- "Why [Common Belief] is a Lie"
- "Stop [Common Practice]"
- "[X] Things I Refuse to Do"

**Voice traits:** Bold, provocative, authentic

**Avoid:** Being diplomatic, hedging

### Hero Content

**Headlines:**
- "How to Conquer [Challenge]"
- "The Battle-Tested Method for [Outcome]"
- "Rise Above [Obstacle]"

**Voice traits:** Empowering, action-oriented, determined

**Avoid:** Victim mentality, excuses

### Magician Content

**Headlines:**
- "Transform Your [X] in [Timeframe]"
- "The Secret to [Transformation]"
- "Unlock Your [Potential]"

**Voice traits:** Inspiring, transformative, mystical

**Avoid:** Small thinking, incremental change

### Caregiver Content

**Headlines:**
- "A Gentle Guide to [Topic]"
- "How to Support [Audience] Through [Challenge]"
- "You're Not Alone in [Struggle]"

**Voice traits:** Warm, supportive, generous

**Avoid:** Harshness, judgment
