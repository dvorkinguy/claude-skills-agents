# Content Formulas

Templates for LinkedIn, newsletters, and video scripts.

---

## LinkedIn Post Templates

### 1. The Hook + Story + Lesson

```
[HOOK - provocative statement]

I learned this the hard way.

[STORY - 3-4 sentences, specific details]

Here's what I realized:

[LESSON - 3-5 bullet points]

[CTA - question or follow prompt]
```

### 2. The Contrarian Take

```
Unpopular opinion: [CONTRARIAN STATEMENT]

Here's why most people get this wrong:

1. [Myth] → [Reality]
2. [Myth] → [Reality]
3. [Myth] → [Reality]

The truth? [YOUR INSIGHT]

Agree or disagree? Let me know below.
```

### 3. The Listicle

```
[NUMBER] [THINGS] that [OUTCOME]:

1. [ITEM] - [1 sentence explanation]
2. [ITEM] - [1 sentence explanation]
3. [ITEM] - [1 sentence explanation]
...

Which one resonates most? Comment below.
```

### 4. The Before/After

```
[TIME] ago, I was [BAD STATE].

Today, I [GOOD STATE].

What changed? [TRANSFORMATION INSIGHT]

Here's the exact process:

Step 1: [ACTION]
Step 2: [ACTION]
Step 3: [ACTION]

[CTA]
```

### 5. The "How I..." Story

```
How I [ACHIEVEMENT] in [TIMEFRAME]:

[CONTEXT - 1 sentence]

Most people think you need [COMMON APPROACH].

I did the opposite.

[YOUR APPROACH - 3-4 sentences]

Result: [OUTCOME WITH NUMBERS]

The lesson: [TAKEAWAY]
```

## LinkedIn Hook Formulas

| Type | Formula | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Pattern Interrupt | "Stop [common action]" | "Stop sending cold emails" |
| Curiosity Gap | "I [did X] and [unexpected Y]" | "I quit my job and made more money" |
| Controversy | "Unpopular opinion: [take]" | "Unpopular opinion: Hustle culture is a scam" |
| Number + Promise | "[X] ways to [outcome]" | "5 ways to double your leads" |
| Question | "Why do [people] always [behavior]?" | "Why do founders always burn out?" |
| Confession | "I was wrong about [topic]" | "I was wrong about remote work" |

## Newsletter Templates

### The Weekly Insight Format

```
Subject: [NUMBER] or [CURIOSITY HOOK]

---

Hey [NAME],

[HOOK - 1-2 sentences that pull them in]

This week, I want to share [TOPIC].

---

## The Insight

[CONTEXT - What prompted this?]

[THE MEAT - Your teaching, 2-3 paragraphs]

[EXAMPLE - Story or case study]

---

## Your Action Item

[ONE SPECIFIC THING they can do this week]

---

[PERSONAL SIGN-OFF]

[NAME]

P.S. [ADDITIONAL VALUE or CTA]
```

### Subject Line Formulas

| Type | Formula | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Number | "[X] things I learned..." | "3 things I learned from failing" |
| Question | "Why [unexpected thing]?" | "Why I stopped setting goals" |
| How-To | "How to [outcome] without [pain]" | "How to get clients without cold calls" |
| Curiosity | "The [adjective] truth about [topic]" | "The uncomfortable truth about success" |
| Personal | "I need to tell you something" | Direct, personal |
| Result | "[OUTCOME] in [TIMEFRAME]" | "$10K in 30 days" |

## Video Script Templates

### YouTube Long-Form (8-15 min)

```
[0:00-0:15] HOOK
"What if I told you [surprising claim]?"
or
"[X] years ago, I [situation]. Today, I [transformation]."

[0:15-0:30] CREDIBILITY
"I'm [name], and I've [credential/experience]."

[0:30-1:00] PROMISE
"In this video, you'll learn [3 specific things].
Stick around until the end for [bonus/surprise]."

[1:00-2:00] CONTEXT
[Why this matters, the problem]

[2:00-8:00] CONTENT
Point 1: [Teaching + Example]
Point 2: [Teaching + Example]
Point 3: [Teaching + Example]

[8:00-9:00] RECAP
"So to summarize..."
[Quick bullet points]

[9:00-END] CTA
"If this was valuable, hit subscribe."
"Drop a comment with [specific question]."
"Check out [related video] next."
```

### Short-Form (30-60 sec)

```
[0:00-0:03] HOOK
[Pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question]

[0:03-0:08] PROMISE
"Here's how to [outcome]"

[0:08-0:45] VALUE
[1-3 quick tips, show don't tell]

[0:45-0:60] CTA
"Follow for more [topic]"
"Comment [X] if you want [Y]"
```

### Podcast Intro (for guest appearances)

```
"Thanks for having me, [HOST].

For those who don't know me, I'm [NAME].
I help [AUDIENCE] achieve [OUTCOME].

Before we dive in, here's a quick story
that explains why I do what I do...

[30-second origin story]"
```

## Engagement Boosters

### LinkedIn CTA Ideas

- "Which of these resonates most? Comment below."
- "Agree or disagree?"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Save this for later (bookmark icon)"
- "Repost to help someone who needs this."
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "Drop a [emoji] if you've been there."

### Newsletter CTA Ideas

- "Hit reply and tell me..."
- "Forward this to someone who needs it"
- "Click here to [specific action]"
- "What should I cover next week?"

## Content Repurposing Flow

```
Long-Form Content (YouTube, Blog)
        ↓
Newsletter (Weekly insight)
        ↓
LinkedIn Post (Key takeaway)
        ↓
Twitter Thread (Step-by-step)
        ↓
Short Video (Best moment)
        ↓
Quote Card (One-liner)
```

One piece of content = 6 pieces of content.
